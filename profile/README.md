# 🏢 OTU Capstone Project

**Welcome to the official GitHub organization for our Ostim Technical University Software Engineering Capstone Project. We are a team of 5 senior engineering students dedicated to building enterprise-grade, AI-driven software solutions.**

---

## 🚀 Corporate Transportation & Operations Management Platform

We are redefining corporate transportation needs in the Turkish and global markets beyond a simple ride-hailing service, transforming it into a fully-fledged **B2B SaaS and Operational Management Layer**.By bringing traditional companies' manually operated processes under digital discipline, we aim to reduce costs and minimize the operational workload.

<br>

### 🏗️ 3 Main Architectural Layers

<div align="left" style="display: inline-block; text-align: left; max-width: 800px;">

Our system is built upon the following three core operational pillars:
1. **Corporate Internal Automation Layer:** The main SaaS dashboard where companies manage their internal transportation policies, multi-level approval workflows, and department-based budgeting.
2. **Vehicle Allocation System:** A complex module where companies manage long-term vehicle rentals, dynamic pricing, vehicle availability tracking, and shift planning.
3. **Point-to-Point Transfer Services:** Real-time tracking and management of operational workflows for direct transfers.

</div>

<br><br>

### 🧠 AI & Engineering Layer (The X-Factors)

<div align="left" style="display: inline-block; text-align: left; max-width: 800px;">

The artificial intelligence modules that differentiate our platform from competitors and transform it into a true technology product:
* **NLP Smart Dispatcher:** A natural language processing layer that automatically extracts all booking data from a single-sentence message via LLM integration.
* **AI Carpooling & Route Optimization:** Smart optimization that identifies employees heading in the same direction using clustering algorithms, providing up to 40% cost savings.
* **Driver Scoring & Safety Index:** An analytics engine that analyzes passenger feedback using NLP Sentiment Analysis and combines it with IoT sensor data to calculate a driving safety score.
